Hello There! Money runs the world, that's no secret. This then leads us to the discussion about Investments. I'm sure that you have heard of the infamous "Bitcoin" and stocks like Gamestop that have been in the news recently. But what really are Investments? Essentially, an investment is the action of funding a certain product or idea over a period of time and eventually (hopefully) getting a return on your funds. Here at Venture Fortuna, our goal is to provide you with the knowledge you need in order to go out and start investing because we believe this knowledge should be free and known by everyone.
